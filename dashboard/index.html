Key takeaways (by brand & platform)

  • Hollister · Reddit (48.9% positive): Negative posts often cite adhesives and leakage; positives mention reliability and support.
  • Coloplast · Reddit (56.7% positive): Higher positive share than Hollister on the same platform (merged CSV).
  • Hollister · UOAA (45.3% positive): Forum threads emphasize adhesive failure and skin barriers (presentation themes).
  • Coloplast · UOAA (47.3% positive, 28.9% negative): More polarizing than Hollister on UOAA in presentation summary.
